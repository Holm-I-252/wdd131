Movie Trivia Site

Ian Holm

WDD 131-2

Overview

Purpose

The site's purpose is to let users play a trivia game about movies.

Audience

Movie lovers, trivia fans.

Dynamic elements

The site will include a question that us fetched from an API, and then multiple choice options for the user to choose their answer. They will be able to track their progress as they play, and see how many they got correct.
